About Me

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Jinshan Pan. Before that, I received my M.S. degree from the College of Electronic Information Engineering, Shenyang Aerospace University, advised by Prof. Yufeng Li. My research interests cover computer vision and deep learning, with special emphasis on low-level vision. Specifically, I am dedicated to designing learning algorithms to solve vision problems under adverse weather (e.g., rain/haze removal). If you want to cooperate with me, kindly contact me via email.
