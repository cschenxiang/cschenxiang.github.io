I'm Hao Tang, a final-year Ph.D. Candidate at Nanjing University of Science and Technology in Intelligent Media Analysis Group (IMAG), supervised by Prof. Jinhui Tang and co-supervised by Prof. Zechao Li. Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree from Harbin Engineering University in June 2018. Currently, I'm a visiting Ph.D. student at Singapore Management University in the Visual Understanding and Generation (VUG) laboratory, working closely with Prof. Shengfeng He. Previously, I held a similar position at Singapore University of Technology and Design in the Computer Vision & Learning Group (VLG), working closely with Prof. Jun Liu. My primary research interests mainly focus on data-efficient Learning and its applications in Computer Vision and Multimedia. The ultimate goal of my research is to develop machines that can learn from Limited, Dynamic, and Imperfect data in real-world scenes like humans.
