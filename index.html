Biography

I am currently a final-year Ph.D. candidate at Intelligent Media Analysis Group (IMAG Lab), School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST), under the supervision of Prof. Jinshan Pan. I also serve as the founder and CEO of EntroVision (熵视科技). My research interests include image processing and computer vision, mainly focusing on image deraining and low-level vision problems. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me (any question / suggestion / collaboration).
